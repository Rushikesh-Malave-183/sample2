☕ How to Make Tea

1. Boil Water

Pour fresh water into a kettle and bring it to a boil.

2. Add Tea Leaves or Tea Bag

Place tea leaves or a tea bag into a cup or teapot.

3. Pour Hot Water

Carefully pour the boiling water over the tea leaves or bag.

4. Steep and Serve

Let it steep for 3–5 minutes, then remove the tea bag or strain the leaves. Add sugar or milk if desired, and enjoy your tea!
